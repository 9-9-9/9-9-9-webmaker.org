Let’s make a meme

A “meme” is something that’s shared on the internet, and it usually has three parts: a picture, a caption, and a dash of humor. In this activity, you’ll make your own meme using a bit of HTML and your own creativity. Thimble, Mozilla’s code editor, makes it easy.
